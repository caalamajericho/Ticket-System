FAQs

Ask us anything

Have any questions? We're here to assist you.

How do I change my account email?

You can log in and change it from Profile > Edit Profile.

What should I do if my payment fails?

Use COD if available or retry. Failed payments will be refunded within 7-10 days.

What is your cancellation policy?

You may cancel an order within 1 hr in processing state. Refunds go to original method.

How do I check order delivery status?

Check "My Orders" in the app or website to view delivery status.

What is Instant Refunds?

Upon successful pickup, you may instantly receive a refund.

Can I update my ticket after submitting it?

Yes, through comments or attachments. Some details may not be editable.
